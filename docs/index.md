# COSC 350 Exam Reviews

Welcome to the COSC 350 (Operating Systems) Exam Review site for Salisbury University!

This site contains supplemental practice questions and review material to help you prepare
for each exam throughout the course.

## How to Use This Site

Navigate using the menu on the left to find review material organized by exam:

- **Midterm 1 Review** – Covers processes, CPU scheduling, and threads.
- **Midterm 2 Review** – Covers synchronization, deadlocks, and memory management.
- **Final Exam Review** – Covers file systems, I/O systems, and virtual machines.

## Tips for Studying

1. Review your lecture notes and textbook readings first.
2. Attempt each practice question on your own before looking at hints.
3. Focus extra time on topics where you feel least confident.
4. Form study groups to discuss difficult concepts.

Good luck on your exams!
